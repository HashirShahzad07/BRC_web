Abstract

Bitcoin, being the trailblazer in blockchain and cryptocurrency, paved the way for subsequent advancements like NFTs, smart contracts, and tokenization. While its rarely-altered protocol brings stability, Bitcoin has been criticized for appearing somewhat stagnant. However, the introduction of Ordinals has breathed new life into Bitcoin's ecosystem, driving swift progress in 2023. Ordinals, a protocol layered onto the Bitcoin blockchain, facilitates the creation of NFTs and BRC-20 tokens without tweaking Bitcoin's code. Similar to Ethereum's ERC-20, the BRC-20 standard supports various token projects, enhancing Bitcoin's appeal for both entertainment and education. BRC-20 tokens are creating a promising landscape for future developments and opportunities, echoing Ethereum's growth in 2017.

BRCStarter aims to do its part on paving the future path of BRC-20 tokens by providing an app allowing the projects to have the ecosystem for their activities
